Description:
With MailPeek you will get a multilingual (English, German, Spanish etc.), small and slick POP3 email client that lets you periodically check an unlimited number of email accounts. You can either download the headers only or the entire message. You can also send emails incl. attachments thru SMTP. MailPeek comes with a simple address book.
