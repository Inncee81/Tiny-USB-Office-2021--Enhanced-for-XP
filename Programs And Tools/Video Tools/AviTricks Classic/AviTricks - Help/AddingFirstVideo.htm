ADDING THE FIRST VIDEO TRACK TO A NEW PROJECT

1. Click the gray “Add video” button at the top of the timeline.

2. Select “Video” and click “OK”.

3. Locate the video on your computer. Click “Open” and then “OK”.

Important Information about the Location of Source Material.

The video file you have added appears in three places. On the preview screen as a video. On the timeline as an orange  bar. And in the tree diagram on the left.



You can now cut the track itself, changing its starting and/or ending point.

You can also add another video track, add special effects to alter video tracks or add transitions between video tracks.
