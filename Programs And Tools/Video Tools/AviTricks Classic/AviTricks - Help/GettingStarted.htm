GETTING STARTED 

At startup AviTricks is automatically ready for a new video project.

Users who have already started a video project and wish to continue working on it will need to open their project first.

New users or users who are starting a new project are ready to go.



In the middle of the screen we see a black hole on a navy blue background. This is the preview screen, on which your video will appear.

Under the preview screen on the right is the timeline.

Under the preview screen on the left is a white block where the tree-structure will appear. It is ready for a “New Project” and a “Video track”.

The first step is to add a video track.
