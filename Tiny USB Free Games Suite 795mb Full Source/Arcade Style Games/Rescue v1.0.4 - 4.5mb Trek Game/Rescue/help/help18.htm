Engineering: Damage Control

The Status Report window gives you an in depth look at the repair status of all of the Enterprise systems. "OK" indicates that the system is functional, while "3 hr rpr" (for example) indicates that the system is damaged and will not be functional for approximately 3 hours. (Each "hour" of repair work takes approximately 10 to 15 seconds of real time to complete.)

Repairs can be Prioritized by going to the Status Report window and clicking directly on the system that you want to be repaired first. A wrench will appear beside the system, and if the system is damaged it will be repaired approximately 2 to 3 times faster than any other systems that are in need of repair.

Notes on Energy Managment

Remember that all Enterprise systems use energy. Here are some ways to minimize your energy intake:

  • Turn off things like the Long Range Scan or Sensor Scan if you're not using them.

  • Shields are especially high consumers and should always be turned off when not required.

  • Keep warp speeds to a minimum comfortable level.

  • Use the Engineering menu (see next page) for additional options.
