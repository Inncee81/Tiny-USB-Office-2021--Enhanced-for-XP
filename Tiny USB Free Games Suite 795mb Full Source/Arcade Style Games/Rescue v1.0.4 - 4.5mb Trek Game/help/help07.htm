The U.S.S. Enterprise

The U.S.S. Enterprise, a heavy cruiser, is the most powerful ship in the Federation. The following ship systems are at your disposal:

  • Warp Drive (for most of your traveling needs)

  • Impulse Drive (for precise short range maneuvering)

  • Photon Torpedoes (powerful, sometimes inaccurate weapon)

  • Phasers (accurate, less powerful weapon)

  • Shields (critical for defense)

  • Transporters (for transporting Away Teams)

  • Ship's Computer (for finding things and making life easier)

  • Away Teams (for evacuating the Federation Outposts)

  • Engineering (for managing energy & repairs efficiently)

The Enterprise has room in its holds to temporarily house ALL of the colonists from ALL of the outposts, until you can get them to safety at a Starbase. It has the destructive firepower to match almost any enemy ship (if used properly) and can outrun almost every enemy as well.
