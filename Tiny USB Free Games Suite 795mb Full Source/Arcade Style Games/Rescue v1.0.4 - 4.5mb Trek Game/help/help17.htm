The Onboard Computer (use the Computer menu):

  • Find: this submenu lets you find a variety of different objects and relay the coordinates to the WARP drive controls, or the IMPULSE drive if it's very close by. All you need then do is click the appropriate ENGAGE button.

  • Chase: when you use this submenu to choose from a variety of targets, the computer will lock on to the nearest object of that type in the galaxy and chase it, using both the Warp and Impulse drives as appropriate, until the Enterprise is right on top of it. You can deactivate Chase mode at any time by clicking on any of the Warp or Impulse drive controls. Chase mode is available only if both the Warp and Impulse drives are operational and the Enterprise has plenty of energy for the chase.

  • Auto Snap-On Shields: When turned on, this option automatically raises the shields whenever an enemy vessel is in close proximity.

  • Auto Select Nearest Enemy: When turned on, this option automatically targets the nearest enemy vessel visible in the Main Viewer. All you need then do is fire your weapons.

  • Set Auto-Destruct: when you've run out of options. After confirming your decision, Set Auto-Destruct blows up the Enterprise.
