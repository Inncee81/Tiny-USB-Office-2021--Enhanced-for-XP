The Romulans are a cunning and intelligent race, and their starship commanders are no less so. Their tactics range from hit and run to head on assault; prepare for anything!

  • Their standard ship is the Warbird, a light cruiser which carries phasers and torpedoes. (Only phasers in the Cadet's Game.) By itself it is no match for the Enterprise (as long as you defend yourself), but in packs they can overwhelm you before you know what's happening!

  • The Romulan SuperHawk is an ultra-modern cruiser. (Does not appear in Cadet's Game.) They are extremely powerful and are equipped with a Cloaking Device which, when turned on, renders them invisible! Though they cannot fire weapons while cloaked, they can maneuver, so be careful! Note: If by chance a cloaked ship is hit, it will most likely become visible again... if only for a moment.

  • There have been reports of Romulan saboteurs on Morlikon 9. (Admiral's Game only.) Be especially alert when visiting this planet...
