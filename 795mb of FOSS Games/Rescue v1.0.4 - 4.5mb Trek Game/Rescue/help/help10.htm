  • The Klingons (dark green), once sworn enemies of the Federation, are now your strong and loyal allies. Their Battlecruisers can be of great value to you, intercepting and attacking enemy ships. The Klingon starship commanders are usually willing to fly their ship alongside the Enterprise for additional firepower, if you ask them to.

  • The Cardasians (light red) have sided with the Romulans in this scenario and thus are deadly enemies. Their Scrorpion ships are smaller and weaker than Romulan warships, but they are just as dangerous, because they home in on your outposts and strike at them very quickly.

  • The Ferengi (dark red) are considered allies, but they will probably be of little use to you since their interest lies in trade and profit, not fighting. In a wartime scenario such as this, their Marauder ships are likely to be found looking for protection near a starbase.
